Waakye Food

Waakye Delicious Food

Waakye is one of the delicacies love by majority of Ghanaians.
This is true because every household in Ghana can not live two
consecutive days without preparing and eating Banku. This makes it
a routine delicacy.

Waakye food is an aged long delicacy in Ghanaian societies.
It is believed that Banku food was first prepared by the Hausa
people when they first arrived in the then Goal Coast.
Since then, 90% of all the tribes in Ghana embraced Banku as stable
food that is prepared and eaten almost everyday.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Shito
  • Flavour
  • Fish

Steps: Waakye Preparation

  1. Get your rice ready
  2. Get your beans ready
  3. Heat water and pour the mixture inside the boiling water
  4. After 30 minutes, add spices
  5. Prepare your Shito
  6. Your Waakye is ready
