Banku Food

My Favourite Food

Banku is one of the delicacies love by majority of Ghanaians.
This is true because every household in Ghana can not live two
consecutive days without preparing and eating Banku. This makes it
a routine delicacy.

Banku food is an aged long delicacy in Ghanaian societies.
It is believed that Banku food was first prepared by the Hausa
people when they first arrived in the then Goal Coast.
Since then, 90% of all the tribes in Ghana embraced Banku as stable
food that is prepared and eaten almost everyday.

Ingredients

  • Maize Flour
  • Okro
  • Groundnut Paste
  • Yura Yura
  • Salmon or Any available fish

Steps: Banku Preparation

  1. Get your maize Flour ready
  2. Prepare the Maize though
  3. Heat water and pour the maize though bit by bit while steering
  4. Taste it with your tongue to ensure it is cooked well
  5. Prepare your soup
  6. Your Banku delicacy is ready
